Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. It is a major cultural and religious center in India, known for its temples and ghats along the River Ganges.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World'. It is located in the Himalayan foothills beside the Ganges River and is a popular destination for yoga, meditation, and adventure activities.

Amritsar

Amritsar is home to the Golden Temple, the holiest gurdwara of Sikhism. It is a place of great spiritual and historical significance, attracting millions of visitors every year.
